# SignFlow Pricing

## Philosophy

We believe e-signatures should be affordable and accessible. Our pricing is transparent, with no hidden fees or surprise charges.

## Plans

### Free
**$0/month**

Perfect for individuals and small teams getting started.

- ✅ Unlimited signatures
- ✅ Up to 3 team members
- ✅ 10 templates
- ✅ Basic audit trail
- ✅ Email notifications
- ✅ Mobile signing
- ✅ PDF upload and signing

**Limitations:**
- SignFlow branding on documents
- No API access
- No custom branding

---

### Professional
**$19/month per user**

For growing teams that need more power and flexibility.

Everything in Free, plus:
- ✅ Unlimited team members
- ✅ Unlimited templates
- ✅ Custom branding (logo, colors, email domain)
- ✅ API access (1,000 calls/month included)
- ✅ Bulk send (up to 500 recipients)
- ✅ Advanced field types (conditional logic, formulas)
- ✅ Webhooks
- ✅ Priority support (24h response)
- ✅ Remove SignFlow branding

**Annual billing:** 2 months free ($190/year per user)

---

### Enterprise
**Custom pricing**

For large organizations with advanced needs.

Everything in Professional, plus:
- ✅ SSO / SAML (Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace)
- ✅ White-label / custom domain
- ✅ Unlimited API calls
- ✅ Advanced analytics
- ✅ Dedicated account manager
- ✅ SLA (99.9% uptime)
- ✅ HIPAA / SOC 2 compliance support
- ✅ On-premises deployment option
- ✅ Custom integrations
- ✅ 4h support response time

## FAQ

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes, Professional tier has a 14-day free trial. No credit card required.

**Q: Can I self-host for free?**
A: Yes! The entire platform is open-source under AGPL-3.0. Self-hosting is completely free.

**Q: What happens if I exceed API limits?**
A: Additional API calls are charged at $0.15 per 1,000 calls, or upgrade to Enterprise for unlimited.

**Q: Can I switch plans?**
A: Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. Prorated billing applies.

**Q: Is there a setup fee?**
A: No setup fees for Professional. Enterprise may have implementation fees for complex deployments.
